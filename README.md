# Privacy Policy for Simple Counter

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Introduction

Simple Counter ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**Simple Counter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

Our app operates entirely on your device. All data (counter values) remains local to your device and is never transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

## Data Storage

All counter data is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data, and it is never synchronized with any cloud services or external servers.

## Permissions

Simple Counter does not require any special permissions:
- No internet access
- No location services
- No camera access
- No contacts access
- No file system access beyond standard app storage

## Third-Party Services

Simple Counter does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Simple Counter is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Google Play Console developer contact information.
